Get Best Sellers List

GET /svc/books/v3/lists/{date}/{list-name}.json
Get the details of a specific Best Sellers list by date and list name.

Get Best Sellers Lists Overview

GET /svc/books/v3/lists/overview.json
Get the top 5 books from all Best Sellers lists for a specific date.

Get All Best Sellers List Names

GET /svc/books/v3/lists/names.json
Get all available Best Sellers list names with their encoded versions and metadata.

Get Book's Best Sellers List History

GET /svc/books/v3/lists/best-sellers/history.json
Search for a book's history on Best Sellers lists by author, ISBN, or title.

Search Book Reviews

GET /svc/books/v3/reviews.json
Search for NYT book reviews by ISBN, title, or author. At least one parameter is required.

Get Age Groups

GET /svc/books/v3/lists/age-groups.json
Get all available age groups for Best Sellers lists.
